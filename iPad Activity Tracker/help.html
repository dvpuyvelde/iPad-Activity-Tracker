iPad Activity Tracker


Select an event from your agenda on the left. This list shows you both events in your iPad calendar as well as those in Salesforce. If a meeting in Salesforce has the same start time and subject as one on your iPad, the list will only show the Salesforce event.

The icons in the event list have the following meaning :

iPad Calendar event not saved to Salesforce

Salesforce event

Salesforce event where 'Related To' OR 'Type' has been filled in

Salesforce event where 'Related To' AND 'Type' has been filled in

Setting Related To and event Type :
Select any event and use the 'Related To' and 'Type' buttons to link your activities to opportunities or accounts.
The opportunities list that shows up consists of all opportunities your are following in Chatter and/or the ones where you are on the Opportunity Team.
If you search for accounts or opportunities to link to, you can use a combination of keywords just as you would in the regular Salesforce search.

Your events will autosave when selecting a 'Related To' or 'Type'. Alternatively, use the Save button to save manually. Deleting a Salesforce event will remove it from Salesforce, deleting an iPad calendar event will remove it from your iPad calendar.
